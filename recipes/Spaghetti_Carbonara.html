Spaghetti Carbonara

Experience the authentic flavors of Italy with our Spaghetti Carbonara recipe.
This traditional dish combines al dente spaghetti with crispy pancetta and a
luxuriously creamy sauce made from egg yolks and Pecorino Romano cheese.
The key to perfect Carbonara is to toss the hot pasta with the egg and cheese
mixture off the heat, ensuring a smooth, silky sauce that coats each strand of spaghetti.
Finished with a generous sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper, this dish is a true testament
to Italian culinary simplicity and excellence.

Ingredients Needed

This is a list of all the required Ingredients for this simple dish.

  • 350g spaghetti
  • 200g guanciale (or pancetta)
  • 4 eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

Follow these steps below to make this wonderful dish.

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Cut the guanciale into small pieces and cook in a skillet until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix with grated Pecorino Romano cheese and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the guanciale in the skillet.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and mix in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce without scrambling the eggs.
  6. Serve immediately, topped with additional Pecorino Romano and black pepper.
