Classic French Crepes

Embrace the versatility and elegance of Classic French Crepes, a staple of French cuisine. These thin, delicate pancakes are made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, water, and melted butter, resulting in a light and airy texture. Crepes can be served with a variety of fillings, from sweet options like Nutella, fresh fruits, and whipped cream to savory combinations such as ham, cheese, and saut√©ed vegetables. The key to perfect crepes is a well-heated non-stick skillet and a thin, even layer of batter. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or dessert, these crepes offer endless possibilities for delightful and sophisticated dining.

Prep Time

5 mins

Cook Time

25 mins

Serves

12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • Additional butter for cooking

Instructions

  1. Make the Batter: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and melted butter, whisking until smooth.
  2. Rest the Batter: Let the batter sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes. This helps to ensure the crepes will be light and tender.
  3. Cook the Crepes:Heat a lightly oiled non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  4. Cook Each Side:Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, flip and cook the other side for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve: Serve crepes warm with your choice of fillings. Classic options include lemon and sugar, Nutella, fresh fruits, or savory fillings like ham and cheese.
