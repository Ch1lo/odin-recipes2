Chicken Tikka Masala

Dive into the vibrant and aromatic world of Chicken Tikka Masala,
a beloved dish that marries tender pieces of marinated chicken with
a rich, spiced tomato and cream sauce. The chicken is first marinated
in a blend of yogurt and spices, then grilled to perfection.
It's then simmered in a sauce made from onions, tomatoes, garlic,
ginger, and a variety of spices including cumin, coriander, and garam masala.
The addition of heavy cream gives the sauce a luscious, velvety texture.
Garnished with fresh cilantro, this dish pairs beautifully with basmati
rice or warm naan bread, making it a perfect choice for a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Ingredients Needed

This is a list of all the required Ingredients for this simple dish.

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground garam masala
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Follow these steps below to make this wonderful dish.

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, and garam masala for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet, add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and cook until the onion is soft.
  3. Add the chili powder and cook for 1 minute, then add the marinated chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the heavy cream and simmer for another 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
